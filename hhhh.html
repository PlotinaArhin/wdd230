Springfield Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Springfield Chamber of Commerce. This name was selected as it directly represents the city where the chamber operates and is easily recognizable to the local community.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Springfield Chamber of Commerce website is to:

  • Promote local businesses through member services and community events.
  • Provide information on economic development and public policies that impact local businesses.

Scenarios

Potential questions from site visitors:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

#1E90FF - Primary color for headings and accents
#FFFFFF - Background color for the site

Typography

Fonts to be used:

  • Headings: Arial, sans-serif
  • Body: Georgia, serif
